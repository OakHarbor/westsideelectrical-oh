Serving Whidbey Island and Oak Harbor

My name is Ryan Thompson and I have been an electrician serving Oak Harbor and the surrounding area since 2013. My grandfather owned Westside Auto Rebuild for many years in Oak Harbor. He was well loved in the community and left a great impression. The name Westside Electrical is derived from that and I hope to continue his legacy this way and serve the community I grew up in.

I completed 5year trade school apprenticeship with the IBEW, recognized by Washington State and nationally. WestSide Electrical is honest and fair, will explain any repairs, why it may have failed, produce photos if needed, and answer any questions you may have at the time. We will make your experience stress free and trust you are in good hands. To play our part in reducing a carbon footprint, we are 98% paperless. Billing and payments can be done on the spot or by email.

Frequently Asked Questions

What cities do you operate in? +

Currently, we are in Toronto, Miami, and Los Angeles. However, we are not limited to these locations only and are always looking to expand to other markets. Please contact us to find out how we can help you no matter your location!

What is a short-term property rental?+

A short-term property rental refers to a lease with a duration of less than six months. Standard residential lease agreements require a one-year commitment.

Why should I allow Park Place Properties to manage my property?+

We can help you save time and grow revenues through short-term rentals. Our team of experts ensure that your listings are promoted on a variety of short-term rental platforms. Customer queries are answered immediately, and listings are optimally priced to take advantage of seasonal demand. Our professional cleaning team ensures that your property is thoroughly cleaned and fully stocked before and after each guest visit. We offer 24/7 concierge services for you and your guest(s).

What type of properties do you accept?+

We accept a wide variety of properties including, but not limited to:

  • Studio Apartments/Condos
  • 1 Bedroom Apartment Condos
  • 2 Bedroom Apartment Condos
  • 3 Bedroom Apartment Condos
  • Houses
  • Townhouses

Does my property have to be furnished?+

No! Your property does not have to be furnished. Our expert staging team and designers can furnish your property for you, at an additional cost.

Do I have to sign an agreement?+

Yes - All clients are required to sign a contract with Park Place Properties. However, we understand that unexpected circumstances may arise, resulting in a request for cancellation. In the event that you so wish to withdraw or terminate your contract with Park Place Properties, we kindly ask clients to provide us with a 30-day notice.

What are the benefits of using Park Place Properties’ Services?+

We accepts a variety of properties including but not limited to:

  • Reach "Superhost" status
  • Ensure guest satisfaction, resulting in 5-star ratings and reviews
  • Dealing with guest inquiries online and in-person
  • Improve your response times
  • Dynamically manage pricing to maximize revenues
  • Promote on other short term rental platforms
  • Generate more views and enhance overall property visibility

If you are interested in finding a tenant or wish to sublet:

  • Generates greater revenue in comparison long-term lease agreements
  • Avoid the hassle of searching and screening tenants
  • Provide professional, reliable, and frequent cleanings
  • Peace of mind that your home is in good hands

What is the onboarding process (What happens after I sign up)?+

Upon signing up for our services, our expert team of staging consultants and photographers will ensure your property is designed to sell. Once the staging process is complete, we will begin to create and populate your property across multiple short-term rental platforms.

How do you ensure guest safety and that my property will be protected?+

Preserving the value of your property as well as the safety of our guests is our number one priority. We have an extremely rigorous guest screening process:

  • Guest Identity Validation. We ensure that guests are correctly identified by requesting valid, government issued identification prior to their stay
  • Guest Analysis. We analyze each review and rating that the guest has previously received
  • Guest Interaction. Once selected as a potential booking, we thoroughly interact with the guest in order to discover: purpose of their travels, size of their party, and any other pertinent information (i.e., "red flags") that will better assist us in finding the ideal booking

What websites will my property be listed on?+

Your property will be listed on (but not limited to) the following websites:

  • Airbnb
  • VRBO
  • Booking.com
  • HomeAway
  • TripAdvisor
  • Expedia

How much money will I make using Park Place Properties services?+

With our Guaranteed Rent Package, you can expect to be paid more than comparable rentals in your area! With our Host Package, you can expect to earn up to 3X more than profits generated through standard leasing agreements. Unique to each client, your revenue will vary depending on the property, location, and seasonality. Further details will be discussed during your consultation with the Park Place Properties team, forecasting your predicted revenue.

How much does your service cost/what are your fees?+

Nothing! If we do not generate any bookings for your property, then you are not required to pay us anything in return.

How much does your service cost/what are your fees?+

Park Place Properties operates on a commission-based model, and on booking fees generated by your property. We charge a 15% fee for services rendered plus an additional cleaning fee, which is absorbed by guests. Utilizing the commission model motivates our team to maximize the revenue that your property generates!

How and when am I paid?+

All payments are made on the last day of every month. We offer multiple forms of payment including: direct deposit, cheque, and Interac e-Transfer.
