Meet The Electrician

My name is Ryan Thompson, owner and operator of Westside Electrical. We are honest and fair, will explain any repairs, why it may have failed, produce photos if needed, and answer any questions you may have at the time. We will make your experience stress free and trust you are in good hands.

From a garage, to a warehouse and everything in between. Weâ€™ve grown with an eye toward the future.

I started off doing mainly residential until I got my residential journeyman license (EL02). I had the desire to learn more of the trade than just residential and wanted to earn my (EL01) journeyman license so I researched other options and got into an inside wireman apprenticeship with the IBEW.

During that time I worked a lot of industrial at the refineries and commercial mixed in as well. Once I finished their school, I wanted to do more and the idea of opening a business was always in my mind. My grandfather owned Westside Auto Rebuild for many years in Oak Harbor. He was well loved in the community and left a great impression. The name Westside Electrical is derived from that and I hope to continue his legacy this way and serve the community I grew up in.

It will just be me for a little while until I can build up enough to expand and bring on more people, but I look forward to what the future has in store and I will do my best to make sure every customer is provided with the best experience they can get.

Why Choose Us As Your Electrician
